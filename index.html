Jan Van Balen

research · notes · thesis · press · talks · github

I’m a machine learning researcher at Spotify. I'm interested in machine learning for audio, music and graphs. In 2020 I spent some time at University of Antwerp. Before that I worked at Apple in London. In 2016, I completed a PhD on at Utrecht University in the Netherlands on data representations for the analysis of audio and music collections.

I have worked on recommender systems, content ID (audio fingerprinting, cover ID, sample ID), music corpus analysis, and other topics in audio signal processing and information retrieval. While at Utrecht University, I helped develop the citizen science games Hooked! (NL) and #hookedonmusic (UK) for a research project on popular music and memory.
